Frequently asked Questions

What exactly does EMAXIQ do?

EMAXIQ is an online consultancy that supports businesses, executives, and projects from initiation to completion. We provide strategic advisory, project capital structuring, executive matching, training, and digital transformation services.

Who are your services designed for?

We work with entrepreneurs, executives, SMEs, investors, and project sponsors across Africa and the diaspora, anyone who needs structured consultancy, funding readiness, leadership support, or project delivery expertise.

How is EMAXIQ different from other consultancies?

We go beyond strategy. At EMAXIQ, we combine advisory with hands-on delivery, matching executives, structuring finance, and tracking performance until results are achieved. Our global experience and African focus make us unique.

Do you only work with businesses in Nigeria?

No. While we are based in Abuja, we serve clients across Africa and the diaspora, with ongoing engagements in the UK, Nigeria, and other markets. Our consultancy model is digital-first, so location is never a barrier.

How can I start working with EMAXIQ?

It’s simple — book a Free Assessment call, request a consultancy brief through our contact page. We’ll assess your needs and recommend the right engagement package, whether strategic advisory, investor readiness, or executive placement.
